Where Europe meets Africa

Tarifa → Tangier · the Strait of Gibraltar ferry

An hour across the narrowest gap where two continents nearly touch. Step off the boat into a labyrinth medina, a clifftop kasbah, and the place that's drawn writers and wanderers for a century.

A desert galaxy & the slave-city of Yunkai

Aït Benhaddou = Yunkai · the desert that doubled for a galaxy far, far away

The mud-brick kasbah outside Ouarzazate has stood in for half of cinema — Daenerys' Yunkai and a sand-blasted world of pod-racers among them.
